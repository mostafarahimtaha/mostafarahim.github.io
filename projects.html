Real-Time Emergency Management Dashboard for South Sinai Governorate (NAS)

This project was developed for the South Sinai Governorate (NAS) as part of the National Emergency and Public Safety Network, delivering an intelligent, real-time dashboard built using Power BI, powered by SQL-based databases and regularly updated CSV data sources. The platform consolidates emergency reports, ambulance dispatches, and infrastructure issues into one seamless interface, while integrating key official contacts and sector-specific metrics. Designed for rapid coordination and situational awareness, it empowers leadership with dynamic visualizations, filters by sector, weather, and time, and supports data-driven decisions to enhance public safety operations across the region.

AI Cane See – Smart Navigation for the Visually Impaired (Graduation Project: Developed a smart AI-powered cane for the visually impaired — awarded an A grade.)

AI Cane See is an intelligent, AI-powered assistant designed to enhance the mobility and independence of visually impaired individuals. Using real-time object detection, voice feedback, and depth sensing, the project provides immediate audio cues to navigate environments safely. Built with Python, YOLOv8, and OpenCV, it integrates computer vision and assistive tech for impactful social good.
